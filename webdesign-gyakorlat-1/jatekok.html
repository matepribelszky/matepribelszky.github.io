The Hidden Fragmentation

The Hidden Fragmentation is a stylish and surreal first person exploration adventure that sees you searching an abandoned facility for a mysterious anomaly called a ‘Fragmentation’.

In The Hidden Fragmentation you find yourself in an dilapidated facility that’s populated by a handful of strange beak-nosed people. You can chat to the people and assist them in different ways, and in most cases they’ll talk about a mysterious anomaly that occurred in the basement. It seems this anomaly is what has caused the facility to fall into disrepair and it’s also why the majority of the survivors are scared to enter the building.

Alagsor

Alagsor is a puzzle game inspired by the popular Snake and Sokoban (and actually a lot of other old games I specially like - Namco's Battle City, for example).

The premise is quite simple (at first sight): you control a snake-like, one eyed creature, which can transform its body to solid wall and the goal is to pass every level - obviously - without dying. Meanwhile you have to collect keys, move boxes, avoid enemies, etc. - and of course the difficulty raises continuously.

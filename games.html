Alagsor

2D puzzle game inspired by Snake, Sokoban and Namco's Battle City. It has been my first and only attempt at creating pixel art graphics and grid-based movement for a video game so far.

The player controls a snake-like, one eyed creature which can transform its body into solid wall to avoid getting caught by enemies. Meanwhile the player needs to collect keys to open locked doors, move boxes, avoid traps, etc.
